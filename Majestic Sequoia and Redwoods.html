Sequoia and Redwood Trees

Sequoia Trees

Sequoias are some of the largest and most fascinating trees in the world!Some can grow to be up to 316 feet tall and some live to be 3,000 years old!

Redwood Trees

Redwood trees are the tallest trees on Earth. Like the Sequoia tree, Redwood trees can live to be 3,000 years old and possibly even older than that.
